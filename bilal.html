CONFERENCE THEMES

Quality Enhancement Practices.

The Sub-Themes are:
  • Good QA practices in imparting knowledge, skills and Research at HEIs.
  • Self-Assessment Reports in facilitating Accreditation Processes.
  • Develop Public Confidence in Higher Education.
  • Competing at International level for seeking standardization of Qualification Framework and employment.
  • Significance of feedback of the stakeholders focusing Employers and Alumni.
  • Challenges in implementation of QA processes.
  • Impact of QA practices in building National Economy.
  • Significance of ISO Certification in promoting Quality Culture in HEIs.

CALL FOR ABSTRACTS

You are invited to present a presentation on a topic of your choice related to one of the topics above. Ideas for topics can be drawn from the list of theme and sub-themes, or can be of your own choice. Please indicate how your selected topic relates to the theme of the Conference.

Guidelines for Abstract submissions
  • Abstract on any topic related to the theme or sub-themes of the Conference will be considered.
  • Abstract should be no more than 250-300 words in length, and a bio-sketch of up to 100 words with one photo of the author.
  • Abstract will be reviewed by a review committee. The acceptance of Abstract for inclusion in the program is at the prerogative of the review committee, and no correspondence will be entered into.
  • If your abstract is selected for presentation, it is expected that you will have: 1)15 minutes for presentation; 2) 5 minutes for questions/discussion.
  • All submissions must be in English.
  • It will be highly appreciated if you could earlier submit your Abstract (in Word Version) to conference@kmu.edu.pk and bukhariqec@kmu.edu.pk at the same time by October 5, 2017
